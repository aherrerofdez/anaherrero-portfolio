GET TO KNOW ME

Passionate about leveraging technology to create impactful products, I'm an experienced Product Manager skilled at driving product development from conception to launch. With a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction and Design from Aalto University and Universidad Politécnica de Madrid, alongside diverse experiences in full-stack development, entrepreneurship, and data science, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and strategic vision to every project. My proficiency spans software development, user experience design, market analysis, and product innovation. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can apply my analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities to drive business growth and deliver exceptional user experiences.

Currently based in Madrid, I've spent over 5 years living abroad in vibrant cities like Beirut and Helsinki, which has cultivated a deep appreciation for diverse cultures. Fluent in English and Spanish, I'm eager to practice my French and currently delving into Arabic. When I'm not geeking out over tech stuff, you'll find me exploring my other passions: music and psychology. Additionally, I'm an avid outdoor enthusiast, enjoying activities like biking, swimming, and hiking. Let's connect and explore collaboration possibilities!
