GET TO KNOW ME

Hey there! I'm a passionate builder, from designing interfaces and writing code to DIY-ing around the house. I am dedicated to leveraging my skills in technology, design, and business to develop innovative products that directly impact individuals, with a particular focus on improving the fields of education and healthcare.

I grew up in Segovia, a historic city in Spain. I studied Information Systems Management at IE University in Madrid, combining my interests in Business Administration and Computer Science. I also spent a semester studying abroad at the American University of Beirut in Lebanon, where I explored the fields of Psychology and Education while eating za'atar manakeesh. This experience, along with my skills in mobile app development, inspired my Bachelor's thesis on using technology to help children with Down Syndrome acquire basic mathematical skills.

During my thesis, I realized the importance of interface design to obtain a great user experience. This led me to pursue a Master's Degree in Human-Computer Interaction and Design, specializing in Accessibility and Adaptability. I completed a dual degree program from the Technical University of Madrid (UPM) and Aalto University in Helsinki, Finland. Additionally, I minored in Entrepreneurship and Innovation, as I have always been interested in the fast-paced world of entrepreneurship. My Master's thesis focused on developing a program that simplifies information according to the easy-to-read guidelines for individuals with cognitive disabilities.
