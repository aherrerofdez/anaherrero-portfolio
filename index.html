GET TO KNOW ME

With a strong background in product management, development, and design across various industries, I bring valuable skills to the Product team. I'm a dynamic and results-driven lifelong learner with a global mindset. I'm committed to improving user experiences, with a special focus on Accessibility and Adaptability. I enjoy creating innovative products that help individuals, particularly in the education and healthcare industries. My diverse skill set spans UX/UI design, web app development, API creation, NLP, and digital marketing, enabling me to bring creative ideas to life and drive growth.

Beyond work, I'm passionate about math, music, developmental psychology, and language learning. I'm fluent in English and Spanish and have professional working proficiency in French. In my free time, I like biking, swimming, tennis, hiking, photography, and mystery novels.
